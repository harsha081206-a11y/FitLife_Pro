Life-Threatening Conditions Caused by Obesity

Heart Disease

Obesity increases heart disease risk by 28%. Excess weight forces your heart to work harder, leading to heart attacks and heart failure.

#1 Killer: Heart disease kills 17.9 million people yearly

Type 2 Diabetes

Being overweight makes you 7x more likely to develop diabetes. This leads to blindness, kidney failure, and amputations.

Warning: 537 million adults have diabetes globally

Stroke

Obesity increases stroke risk by 64%. A stroke can cause permanent brain damage, paralysis, or death within minutes.

Every 40 seconds: Someone has a stroke in the US

GOOD NEWS: IT'S REVERSIBLE!

Losing just 5-10% of your body weight can:

  • Reduce heart disease risk by 50%
  • Cut diabetes risk by 58%
  • Lower blood pressure significantly
  • Improve sleep and energy levels
